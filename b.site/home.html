KOENIGSEGG  CCXR

Swedish Hypercar  ·  Beyond All Limits

Welcome


The Koenigsegg CCXR is one of the most powerful production cars ever built. Engineered in Sweden with obsessive precision, it produces over 1,004 horsepower running on biofuel — making it the first street-legal car to break the 1,000 hp barrier on renewable energy.

Every single car is hand-assembled at the Ängelholm factory by a small team of dedicated engineers. Fewer than 50 units are produced each year.

About The Car

The CCXR Trevita Edition

One of only 2 cars ever completed

Engine

4.7-litre twin-supercharged V8 producing 1,004 hp on E85 biofuel.

Carbon Body

The Trevita edition features a unique diamond weave carbon fibre that appears white and shimmering in sunlight — a process invented exclusively for this model.

Production note:

Originally planned as 3 cars, only 2 were completed due to the extreme difficulty of the manufacturing process.
